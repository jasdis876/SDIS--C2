Component II – STI Clinical Management, Syndromic Management, and Contact Investigation Application in Jamaica

A mini STI handbook for Contact Investigators, focused on clinical syndromes, organisms, anatomy, treatment concepts, partner services, reporting, and safe CI application.

Completion of this component does not certify the learner as a Contact Investigator. It is an introductory/refresher learning activity designed to support understanding of STI prevention, syndromic management, partner services, surveillance, and disease intervention practice in Jamaica.

Completion requirement: Review all component content, complete the final 25-question assessment, achieve a score of at least 80%, and download the awarded 1 CME / 1 learning-hour certificate.
Authority note: Clinical treatment must follow current MOHW STI guidance. Field investigation, documentation, confidentiality, partner services, priority conditions, and case closure must follow the MOHW Contact Investigation Field Guide and supervisor direction.
